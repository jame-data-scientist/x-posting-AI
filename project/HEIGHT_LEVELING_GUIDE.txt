────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1.1  WHAT IS Height Leveling?
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Height Leveling is a cross-platform mobile application (built with Flutter)
that helps young people — primarily teenagers and young adults — maximize their
natural height potential through science-backed daily habits.

The app acts as a personal growth optimization coach. It tracks height over time,
helps users follow a structured daily protocol of exercises, nutrition, sleep, and
supplementation, then uses artificial intelligence to analyze data and forecast
future height outcomes.

The core belief behind the app is that while genetics set a ceiling on height,
most people never reach their true genetic potential because of poor sleep,
bad nutrition, lack of exercise, and other lifestyle factors. Height Leveling addresses this.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1.2  WHO IS IT FOR?
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Primary Users:
  - Teenagers and young adults (ages 13–23) who are still in or near their
    growth phase and want to maximize their natural height.
  - Parents who want to help their children optimize their growth potential.
  - Fitness-conscious individuals who want data-driven insight into their
    physical development.

Secondary Users:
  - People interested in body composition optimization (nutrition, sleep, hormones).
  - Anyone tracking long-term health metrics with an interest in gamification
    and achievement systems.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1.3  THE PROBLEM Height Leveling SOLVES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Most people trying to grow taller have no clear system. They might hear advice
like "drink milk" or "do stretches" but have no way to:

  1. Track whether they are actually growing.
  2. Know which habits have the highest impact on natural height.
  3. Understand what nutrients they are deficient in.
  4. Stay consistent with a proven daily routine.
  5. Get an AI-powered forecast of where they will end up.
  6. Be held accountable through streaks and achievements.

Height Leveling solves all six of these problems in a single, cohesive, gamified app.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1.4  CORE PHILOSOPHY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The app is built around four evidence-based pillars of height optimization:

  PILLAR 1 — SLEEP
    Human Growth Hormone (HGH) is primarily released during deep sleep
    (specifically stages 3 and 4). Proper sleep (8+ hours, consistent schedule,
    correct posture) is the single most impactful factor for natural HGH
    production. The app tracks sleep quality, encourages good sleep hygiene,
    and rewards users who maintain consistent high-quality sleep streaks.

  PILLAR 2 — NUTRITION
    Bone growth and density require a very specific nutritional foundation.
    The most critical nutrients are:
      - Calcium (bone matrix building)
      - Vitamin D (calcium absorption)
      - Vitamin K2 (directs calcium to bones, not arteries)
      - Magnesium (bone flexibility)
      - Zinc (cell growth and protein synthesis)
      - Protein (muscle and bone tissue repair)
      - Boron (extends Vitamin D half-life, supports testosterone)
      - Omega-3 (reduces inflammation, supports bone repair)
      - Collagen/Glycine (bone matrix and connective tissue)
    The app tracks 30+ individual nutrients with scientific daily targets.

  PILLAR 3 — EXERCISE
    Specific types of exercise stimulate growth:
      - Decompression exercises (dead hangs, stretches) elongate the spine.
      - High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) and sprinting trigger natural
        HGH and IGF-1 (Insulin-like Growth Factor) spikes.
      - Jump training stimulates bone remodeling.
      - Postural exercises reduce spinal compression.
    The app provides a full exercise library and daily workout plans.

  PILLAR 4 — SUPPLEMENTATION
    Targeted supplements fill nutritional gaps:
      - Vitamin D3 + K2 combination
      - Magnesium glycinate (also improves sleep quality)
      - Zinc
      - Boron
      - Ashwagandha (reduces cortisol which suppresses HGH)
      - Collagen peptides
    Users can toggle on/off supplements and track their daily intake.

2.1  ONBOARDING & QUESTIONNAIRE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

When a user opens the app for the first time, they go through an onboarding
flow to establish their personal baseline. This includes:

  Collected Data:
    - Full name
    - Date of birth (to calculate age and growth stage)
    - Gender (Male / Female)
    - Country of origin
    - Current height (in cm)
    - Goal height (the height they want to reach)
    - Father's height (in cm)
    - Mother's height (in cm)
    - Current weight (for BMI and BMR calculations)
    - Fitness level (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)

  Why This Data Matters:
    - Father's and mother's height are used to calculate the MID-Parental
      predicted height — a medical estimate of the user's true genetic
      height ceiling.
    - Age determines how many growth years are remaining.
    - Weight enables accurate calorie and macronutrient targets.
    - Fitness level personalizes the exercise recommendations.

  Calculations Performed from This Data:
    - Predicted height: ((Mother + Father + Current) / 3) + (23 - age) x 2
    - BMI: Weight / (Height in meters)²
    - BMR (Mifflin-St Jeor): Base metabolic rate for calorie targets
    - TDEE: Daily calorie expenditure (BMR x 1.5 activity multiplier)
    - Deficit/surplus to goal: How many cm remain to the goal height

2.2  HOME SCREEN (DASHBOARD)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The Home Screen is the primary daily view. It gives the user a complete snapshot
of their progress at a glance.

  Key Sections:

  Weekly Calendar Strip
    - Shows the last 5 days and today in a 7-day horizontal strip.
    - Each day shows a completion indicator:
        * Grey dashed border = habit data exists but incomplete
        * White solid border = partially completed
        * Green border = fully completed (Green Day)
    - Future dates are greyed out.
    - Drives the habit tracking feedback loop daily.

  Current Height vs. Goal Card
    - Displays the user's current logged height in large format (e.g., "167.3 cm").
    - Shows the goal height alongside (e.g., "175 cm").
    - Shows how many cm remain to the goal (e.g., "7.7 cm away").
    - Shows total gain since tracking started (e.g., "+1.2 cm total").
    - Shows monthly growth rate (e.g., "+0.3 cm/month").
    - A linear progress bar shows percentage completion toward the goal
      with an estimated achievement date based on current rate.
    - A live mini line chart plots the height journey over the past 6 months.

  Recent Logs Section
    - Lists the 3 most recent height log entries with dates and delta changes.
    - Each entry shows the exact height value and whether it went up or down
      (green for gain, red for decrease).

  Log Height Button
    - A prominent call-to-action button at the center of the Home Screen.
    - Tapping it opens a dialog where the user enters today's measured height.
    - The app calculates the change from the previous log and stores the entry.
    - Users are encouraged to measure at the same time of day (ideally morning,
      when the spine is at full decompressed height).

  Sleep Tracker Card
    - Shows how many days of "proper sleep" (rated 80+) the user has logged.
    - A morning check-in button prompts users to rate last night's sleep quality.
    - Scientific note: Sleep rated 80+ correlates with adequate HGH release.
    - Once sleep is logged for the day, the button changes to a confirmation state.

  Predicted Height Card (MID-Parental)
    - Shows the AI-calculated genetic height ceiling based on parental heights
      and current age.
    - Informs the user of the gap between their current height and genetic potential.
    - Uses the mid-parental formula: standard medical height prediction method.

  Stat Chips
    - "Total Gain": Cumulative height gained since first log.
    - "To Goal": Centimeters remaining to the user's stated goal.

  AI Insights Promo Banner
    - Prompts users to upgrade to the Pro plan ($20/month) to unlock real-time
      AI blocking factor analysis and personalized guidance.

2.3  TODAY'S PROTOCOL SCREEN (DAILY TASK CHECKLIST)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This screen is the heart of the daily habit engine. It displays a structured
protocol — a personalized daily checklist of growth-optimizing actions.

  Structure:
    Tasks are organized into 4 time groups:
    - MORNING   (6:00 AM – 12:00 PM)
    - AFTERNOON (12:00 PM – 6:00 PM)
    - NIGHT     (6:00 PM – 9:30 PM)
    - CUSTOM    (user-created tasks at any time)

  Protocol Task Examples (Morning):
    - Wake up at the same time every day
    - Drink 500ml of water immediately upon waking
    - 15 minutes of morning sunlight exposure
    - Dead hang (spinal decompression, 30 seconds x 2)
    - Take Vitamin D3 + K2 supplement
    - Eat a calcium-rich breakfast

  Protocol Task Examples (Afternoon):
    - High protein lunch
    - Workout session (from the exercise library)
    - Boron supplement
    - 2L water intake checkpoint

  Protocol Task Examples (Night):
    - Magnesium glycinate before bed
    - Dark/cool room for sleeping
    - No screens 1 hour before bed
    - Sleep on back without thick pillow (reduces spinal compression)

  Features:
    - Tap any task to mark it complete — it gets a checkmark and is struck through.
    - A progress counter at the top shows "X / Y tasks completed".
    - A progress bar with individual dot-segments visualizes daily completion.
    - Each task can have an optional info tag (tapping an info icon shows a
      detailed explanation of why that task matters for growth).
    - Users can add custom tasks with optional info descriptions.
    - Users can delete their custom tasks.
    - A date picker allows reviewing past protocol completion history.
    - A day counter chip shows which day of the program the user is on.
    - A score badge (e.g., "72/100") shows today's protocol adherence score.

  Rotating Growth Tips Carousel:
    At the bottom of the screen, a horizontally auto-scrolling carousel
    displays science-backed growth optimization tips, refreshing every 25 seconds.
    Examples of tips:
      - "Sleeping on your back without a pillow reduces spinal compression by 0.5cm."
      - "Cartilage is 80% water. Hydration keeps spinal discs thick."
      - "High-intensity sprints trigger natural HGH and IGF-1 spikes."
      - "Boron increases Vitamin D half-life and reduces inflammation for bone remodeling."
      - "K2 ensures calcium builds bones rather than clogging arteries."

2.4  DIET & NUTRITION SCREEN
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The Diet screen is a full-featured nutritional tracker specifically optimized
for height growth, tracking far more nutrients than a standard fitness app.

  Food Logging:
    - Users search and log foods from the USDA FoodData Central database
      (the gold standard nutritional database with 300,000+ foods).
    - Portions can be adjusted using grams or other units (e.g., slices, cups).
    - Multiple food items can be logged per day.
    - Logged foods persist per date — users can view past diet logs.

  Nutrients Tracked (30+ data points):
    Macros:
      - Calories, Protein, Carbohydrates, Fat, Fiber

    Key Minerals:
      - Calcium, Iron, Magnesium, Phosphorus, Potassium
      - Sodium, Zinc, Copper, Manganese, Selenium, Boron

    Key Vitamins:
      - Vitamin A, B1 (Thiamin), B2 (Riboflavin), B3 (Niacin)
      - B5 (Pantothenic acid), B6, B9 (Folate), B12
      - Vitamin C, D, E, K

    Special Nutrients:
      - Cholesterol, Omega-3, Choline, Iodine, Fluoride

    Hydration:
      - Daily water intake (in ml)

  Nutrient Display:
    - Visual progress bars for each nutrient against the daily target.
    - "Key nutrients" (most important for height growth) are highlighted.
    - Progress is shown as both a bar and a percentage of goal.

  Supplement Panel:
    - A list of recommended supplements with toggle switches.
    - Each supplement shows dosage, timing, and brief description.
    - Users can mark supplements as "taken" each day.
    - Supplements marked as "Key" are shown prominently.
    - Examples: Vitamin D3, K2, Magnesium, Zinc, Boron, Ashwagandha, Collagen

  Calorie Details:
    - A dedicated sub-screen showing the complete breakdown of logged calories
      for the day, with macronutrient split.
    - Comparison to TDEE (total daily energy expenditure) shows if the user
      is eating enough to support growth.

  Diet Recipes:
    - A curated collection of height-optimizing recipes.
    - Each recipe shows full ingredient list with scalable portions.
    - Nutritional breakdown per serving is calculated automatically.
    - Recipe instructions are step-by-step.
    - Serving size slider allows scaling the recipe up or down.
    - Tapping a recipe allows logging it directly to today's food intake.

2.5  EXERCISE SCREEN
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The Exercise screen provides a complete structured workout system designed
specifically around movements that support maximal height growth.

  Weekly Schedule:
    - A 7-day view showing which workout is planned for each day.
    - Days marked as completed vs. today vs. upcoming.
    - Each day shows the workout type and a brief subtitle.

  Exercise Library:
    Categories of exercises included:
      - Decompression & Stretching (spinal decompression dead hangs,

      - Jump Training (box jumps, rope skipping, squat jumps — stimulate
        bone remodeling and growth plate activity)
      - HIIT / Sprint Work (triggers HGH and IGF-1 hormone spikes)
      - Posture Correction (anterior pelvic tilt fixes, rounded shoulder
        exercises, core strengthening)
      - Yoga / Pilates (full spinal elongation and flexibility)
      - Strength (compound movements to support bone density)

  Each Exercise Entry Includes:
    - Exercise name
    - Duration or repetition count
    - Intensity level (Low / Medium / High)
    - Brief description of benefits for height growth
    - Completion checkbox

  Recovery Section:
    - Lists recovery protocols between workouts.
    - Examples: Cold/hot contrast shower, foam rolling, adequate nutrition
      post-workout, magnesium bath soaks.

  Exercise Logging:
    - Users can mark individual exercises as completed.
    - The app tracks which days had an exercise session for streak calculations.

2.6  INSIGHTS SCREEN (AI-POWERED ANALYTICS)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The Insights screen is the most advanced section of the app. It synthesizes
all user data and uses AI (DeepSeek/LLM API) to produce personalized analysis.

  Level & XP System:
    - Users earn XP (experience points) by unlocking achievements.
    - XP accumulates into levels (250 XP per level).
    - A level badge is displayed prominently at the top of the Insights screen.
    - Progress bar shows XP toward the next level.

  AI Blocking Factor Analysis (Weekend Feature):
    - Every weekend, the app sends the user's weekly data to an AI model.
    - The AI identifies the top 3 "blocking factors" — specific habit failures
      that are most limiting the user's growth potential.
    - Each blocking factor includes:
        * A descriptive title (e.g., "Insufficient Calcium Intake")
        * The user's current stat (e.g., "Only 800mg/day vs 2500mg target")
        * An actionable recommendation
        * An impact severity rating: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
        * A color indicator (red = critical, orange = warning, green = good)
    - This is the core value proposition of the Pro tier subscription.

  6-Month AI Height Forecast:
    - Based on historical height logs and monthly reports, the AI projects
      the user's height trajectory for the next 6 months.
    - The forecast chart shows:
        * Past data points (actual measured heights) — solid line
        * Projected data points (AI prediction) — dashed/different style
    - The AI returns a projected final height and estimated target month.
    - This motivates users by showing a concrete number and timeline.

  Monthly Reports:
    - On the last day of each month, the app generates an AI-powered
      monthly summary report.
    - The report is generated from actual tracked data:
        * Days with 8h+ sleep
        * Days with exercise logged
        * Days with nutrition tracked
        * Average calcium and protein intake
        * Actual height growth recorded in cm
    - The AI returns a structured report card with:
        * Total cm gained that month
        * Overall habit consistency score (0–100%)
        * Estimated percentile ranking vs. other users optimizing for height
    - Reports accumulate over months to build a history timeline.

  Achievements System:
    - 30+ achievement badges organized into categories:
        GROWTH Achievements:
          • First Log — Log your height at least once (10 XP)
          • +0.5cm Club — Gain 0.5cm above starting height (150 XP)
          • +1cm Club — Gain 1cm above starting height (100 XP)
          • +3cm Club — Gain 3cm above starting height (300 XP)
          • +5cm Milestone — Gain 5cm above starting height (500 XP)

        STREAK Achievements (Green Mission Path — all habits done):
          • 3-Day Streak (20 XP)
          • 7-Day Streak (50 XP)
          • 14-Day Streak (100 XP)
          • 30-Day Streak (200 XP)
          • 100-Day Streak (1000 XP)

        SLEEP Achievements:
          • Early Bird — 3 days of proper sleep (25 XP)
          • Sleeper Pro — 14 days (75 XP)
          • 8 Hours Club — 30 days (50 XP)
          • Deep Rest — 60 days (100 XP)
          • Circadian King — 100 days (250 XP)

        NUTRITION Achievements:
          • Calcium King — 2500mg/day for 50 days (50 XP)
          • Protein Power — 150g/day for 50 days (50 XP)
          • Hydrated — 2000ml water/day for 50 days (25 XP)
          • Vitamin D — Hit target for 50 days (50 XP)
          • Balanced Diet — Track nutrients for 50 days (100 XP)

        EXERCISE Achievements:
          • 3-Day Exercise Streak (20 XP) through
          • 365-Day Exercise Streak (1500 XP)

    - Achievements can be filtered by category.
    - Unlocked achievements are permanently stored.
    - New achievements unlock in real-time as thresholds are crossed.

    - Unlocked achievements
    - Monthly reports

  Design:
    - Dark theme (deep background with white/grey typography)
    - Accent color scheme (warm gold/amber tones)
    - Material Design components
    - Emoji-forward UI for friendly, gamified feel
